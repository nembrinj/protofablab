# Hardware

## Turtlebot 3

- Lidar
- Raspberry Pi
- ...

## Motor

- Stepper motor (Whadda WPI401)
- Motor driver (ULN2003)
- Arduino ESP32-S3-Mini (coonected to battery pack)
    - Connect motor via breadboard

## Light

- Light (with builtin Arduino ESP32-C3-MINI-1)

## Battery

- External battery pack (can power 2 devices)

## Custom pieces -- 3D printed or laser cut

- Base (Turtlebot 3 Waffle Plate)
    - Rods to attach to current top layer of robot
- Light casing
- Motor casing
- Rotating piece (attached to motor)
    - Pins to attach scenes
- Paper scene
